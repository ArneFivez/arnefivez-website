Dembe - Short Film (FR)

Dembe is a shortfilm directed by Yassine Hayani.


This film tells the story of Dembe, a teenage boy aged 16, who migrated to Belgium with his mother at the age of two. The two of them resided in a tiny castle, where they shared a room with other immigrants from Senegal. However, Dembe's life takes an unfortunate turn when his mother vanishes unexpectedly, and he is placed with his first foster family at the tender age of four.


Today (2015) Dembe no longer counts the number of foster homes he has had. Despite the hardships he has faced, he remains resilient and holds onto the hope that he will one day achieve a sense of stability in his life. He dreams of the day when he can settle down and no longer be haunted by the recurring question that keeps him up at night.


The audience witnesses the initial encounter between him and his new foster mother, Maude. Their first conversation within the new home establishes the groundwork for their relationship moving forward.
