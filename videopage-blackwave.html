(Live Session)

The Official Live Session for the song ‘a-okay’ by blackwave. feat. Abhi The Nomad from their last album ‘no sleep in LA’ directed by Ante Pask.

This live session was recorded at the Bosuil Stadium in collaboration with FIFA23 and RAFC (Royal Antwerp Football Club).
