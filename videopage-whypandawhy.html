Live Session - Why Panda, Why?

A 5-piece live session with music from their debut EP 'Excess' directed by Egon Van Durme.

'Why Panda, Why?' produced a cinematic live session to enhance the music from their debut EP. By visualizing their creative process during composition, they added a new dimension to the project, enriching the experience for their fans. Through this live session, the artists offer a glimpse into their creative minds, providing a sneak peek for their audience.
