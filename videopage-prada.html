Prada - Short Film (NL)

Prada is a shortfilm directed by Jonas Jordens.

The story follows an elderly couple, Maria and François, who have been married for over 50 years. Unfortunately, Maria has been suffering from dementia for some time and often forgets things that have recently happened. However, she still has a deep affection for her cat, Prada, who has been her faithful companion for years.


One night, she wakes up in the middle of the night and starts looking for Prada on the street. François wakes up to find Maria missing and goes outside to look for her. He finds her walking down the street, calling out Prada’s name. He brings her back home and comforts her. After showing her the necklace of her cat, she realizes what has happened.
