Spectrum (EP) - Meyy (Music Videos)

Official music videos for the debut EP 'Spectrum' from rising artist 'Meyy' directed by Arthur Herbosch, Arne Fivez and Marie Janssens.

Four music videos for the songs: Words Like Weapons, Common Love, Shiny Blue Of The Heavens and Sun. Two of them (Shiny Blue Of The Heavens and Sun) were directed by her childhood friend Marie Janssens. The other two (Words Like Weapons and Common Love) were directed by Arthur Herbosch and Arne Fivez.


Although each video evokes a unique atmosphere, they all share a common thread through their sensual visuals.
