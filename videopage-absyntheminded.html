Sunday Child - Absynthe Minded
(Music video)

The Official Music Video of the first single from the upcoming Absynthe Minded album directed by Egon Van Durme & Arne Fivez.

The music video conveys the story of how artists deal with their artistic pursuits. It takes us on a journey through the artist's laboratory, where we witness the main character in five different versions of himself, each engaged in their unique activities. As we gradually uncover each character, we gain insights into the individual behind the artistic persona of ‘Absynthe Minded’. The slow-motion effect serves as a reminder to remain mindful and grateful. The stunning combination of music and imagery results in a piece that transcends the boundaries of a typical music video, offering new discoveries with each viewing.
