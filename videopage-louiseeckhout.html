Live Session - Juxtaposition

Live Session for Louis Eeckhout's research project for the Royal Conservatory Of Antwerp directed by Arne Fivez.

Louis Eeckhout is currently researching the music of jazz piano legend Keith Jarret as part of his latest project. Alongside his bandmates, they have presented two captivating songs. The first is a brilliant interpretation of the free jazz classic 'Jamaican Farewell' by Roscoe Mitchell, while the second is an original composition by Louis himself, titled 'El Coenseliero'. This quartet performs with such seamless harmony and synergy that their renditions sound as if they've been playing together for years. This energy is particularly evident in their stunning execution of 'Jamaican Farewell'. It was recorded in a basement studio in the Royal Conservatory Of Antwerp.
