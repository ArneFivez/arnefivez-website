CD Release - Zefiro Torna

A small teaser for the release of Zefiro Torna's new CD directed by Egon Van Durme.

Zefiro Torna, a music ensemble of global recognition, has been renowned for its inventive and distinctive ideas since its establishment in 1996. The ensemble collaborates with outstanding musicians from various parts of Europe to bring to life musical treasures from the Middle Ages to the Baroque in a way that is unparalleled for a broad audience. By combining historical performance practices with other music genres, literature, philosophy, science, contemporary visual arts, theatre or dance, Zefiro Torna creates captivating total concepts surrounding various symbolic and allegorical themes. This teaser has been designed to promote their latest CD 'Time Crawls', featuring compositions by flautist Jowan Merckx.
