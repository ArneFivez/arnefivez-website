Daydreamers - Short Film (NL)

Daydreamers is a shortfilm directed by Ante Pask.

The film revolves around a father (Jurgen Delnaet) and his daughter (Flo Martens) who share a deep love for motorcycles. Unfortunately, their passion is threatened by the father's eye condition. Throughout the movie, we witness their journey as they navigate this challenging situation. Following their visit to the eye specialist (Robbie Cleiren), it becomes apparent that their relationship will never be the same. However, with the help of his intelligent and innovative daughter, the father finds a way to overcome his struggles.
