Altijd Onderweg - Froze feat. Rick de Vik (Music Video)

Official music video for the song 'Altijd Onderweg' by Froze feat. Rick de Vik directed by Basil Crollet.

Fritz and Robbe, longtime friends bonded by their shared passion for music, have teamed up to produce a Dutch hip-hop track. Froze flowing on the beat, Rick de Vik with the vocal heat. Their brotherhood shines through in every note of the song. This dynamic duo was made to perform together and their collaboration has resulted in an outstanding track.
